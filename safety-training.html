Safety and training

Standards-based training is central to the MU-2 community.

The MU-2 operating culture is built around discipline, recurrent training, strong maintenance standards, and current aircraft-specific knowledge.

SFAR context

Standardized initial and recurrent training changed the aircraft’s safety story.

The MU-2 is subject to special training requirements that call out what training must be accomplished and to what standard. The Association supports the owner-pilot culture that treats recurrent, standards-based training as part of responsible operation.

  • Initial and recurrent training remain core to safe MU-2 operation.
  • Owner-pilots benefit from a peer network that reinforces disciplined procedures.
  • Members can use the forum and Association Q&A to find practical guidance and current contacts.
