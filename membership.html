Membership

Join the MU-2 Flyers Association.

Membership connects you with the people, resources, events, and technical knowledge that make MU-2 ownership and operation stronger.

Request Membership Visit Forum

Benefits

What members receive.

Personalized expert assistance

Board experts help connect members with MU-2 resources, including the people and organizations most likely to know the answer.

Member Q&A

Submit questions to Association experts and benefit from answers shared through member channels and event resources.

Event discounts and resources

Members receive discounts to Association-hosted events, including the annual conference, and access to post-event presentations and expert answers.

Forum, email list, and member tools

The forum is the primary community space and email-list experience, supported by member interaction tools such as member maps.

How to join

Email signup now, Stripe later.

The first version routes membership requests through email. A hosted Stripe membership/payment flow can be added later without rebuilding the site structure.

  • Send your name, email, and connection to the MU-2.
  • Include aircraft make/model, tail number, and serial number if applicable.
  • The Association will follow up with membership next steps.
