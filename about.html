About the Association

A practical type club for the MU-2 operating community.

The MU-2 Flyers Association exists to strengthen the network of owners, pilots, instructors, maintainers, service providers, and advocates who keep the aircraft safe, useful, and well understood.

Purpose

Built around the realities of operating a serious turboprop.

The MU-2 rewards disciplined training, careful maintenance, and a strong peer network. The Association gives owners and pilots a focused place to ask questions, find resources, meet the community, and learn from people with long experience in the type.

  • Connect owners and pilots with knowledgeable MU-2 resources.
  • Promote high standards for training, maintenance, and operating discipline.
  • Preserve useful articles, documents, and community history.
  • Support live events, expert Q&A, and member-to-member interaction.

Who it serves

Focused first on current owners and pilots.

The wider MU-2 ecosystem matters, but the first audience is the owner-pilot community that makes operating decisions every day.

Owners and operators

Practical support for ownership decisions, service resources, event access, and aircraft-specific questions.

Pilots and instructors

A forum, resource archive, and event program centered on safe, current, standards-based operation.

Service community

A bridge to maintainers, product support, parts sources, insurers, and people who understand the type.

Membership is the best way to plug in.

Members get the strongest access to expert help, event resources, community tools, Q&A, and the forum/email-list hub.
