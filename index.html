Membership

Expert help, real relationships, and a better operating network.

Membership is designed for current MU-2 owners and pilots who want direct access to the community, event resources, expert Q&A, and the people who know the aircraft.

Board-guided assistance

Personalized help from board experts who can connect you with trusted MU-2 resources.

Expert Q&A

Members can submit questions and benefit from answers shared through Association channels.

Event advantages

Member discounts for Association events, plus post-event presentations and expert answers.

Forum and email list

Community

The new forum is the center of member interaction.

The Association uses a dedicated Forum as the primary place for MU-2 discussion, expert exchange, announcements, and email-list style participation. Members can follow conversations from the web, mobile app, or by email.
